Get Started with EMS Kiosk (V44)

The EMS Kiosk is an optional module for EMS. You can use the kiosk to view the today’s events, make a reservation for yourself, view your existing reservations, locate a person who has reservations for today, and, if enabled, check yourself into and out of a building.
