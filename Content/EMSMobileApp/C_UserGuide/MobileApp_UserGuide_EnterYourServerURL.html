Enter Your Server URL

You can view and/or change the API URL to which your EMS Mobile App points in one of two ways:

  1. From the Sign in screen, tap About in the lower right-hand corner.

  2. Once you are signed in, tap on the menu and navigate to the About screen. 

  3. Either option will allow you to view, enter, or change the URL. Check with your Administrator for the correct URL.

  4. Tap Connect to finalize the new entry.
