End a Meeting Early

If your meeting has taken less than the reserved time, you can End a meeting early to release the space for others. 

You can End a Meeting early in one of two ways:

  1. From the Calendar on the Home page, locate the meeting you want to End Now.
    1. If you are able to End the meeting, an End Now option will appear when you swipe right. The meeting organizer or booking template usually determines whether this option is available.

  2. You can also End a meeting by opening and/or editing an event. 
    1. Tap on the event and click Edit in the upper right-hand corner. Click End Now.
