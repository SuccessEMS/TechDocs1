Payment Displayed as Incomplete Regardless of Status on CyberSource Gateway

Regics showed payment as "incomplete" even though the payment was complete on CyberSource Gateway. No error logs were generated and no popups were blocked on the CyberSource Gateway.

Fix: Modified functionality so that payments completed on CyberSource Gateway are shown as complete in Regics.
