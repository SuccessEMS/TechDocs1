Outlook > Edit Recurring Meeting

Duplicate Notifications Sent in Error for Edits to Recurring Meetings

When editing recurring reservations in Outlook, multiple, duplicate notifications triggered in error (one for each meeting).

Fix: Modified email triggers so that only one notification generates in this situation.
