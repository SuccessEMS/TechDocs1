Filter by Area

Unable to Filter by Area When Creating/Editing Reservations or Favoriting Rooms

When creating/editing reservations or favoriting rooms, users were unable to filter by area. The building list was empty.

Fix: Modified functionality so that when users filter by area, the building list will display the appropriate buildings for the filtered area.
