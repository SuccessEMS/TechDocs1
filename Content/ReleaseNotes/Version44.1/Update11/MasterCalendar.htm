UDF

UDF Deletion on DTC Was Not Reflected in MC After Connector Purge and Run

Deletion of UDFs from reservations in the EMS Desktop Client were not reflected in the reservations in EMS Master Calendar. This was occurring if the connector included the UDF filter.

Fix: Modified functionality so that removing UDFs from reservation in EMS Desktop Client will remove the same UDFs from the reservation in EMS Master Calendar.
