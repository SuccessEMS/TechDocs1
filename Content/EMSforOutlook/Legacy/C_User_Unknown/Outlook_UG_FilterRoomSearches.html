Filtering Room Searches

You can filter rooms shown on the List (available rooms) and Schedule (all rooms) tabs. From either tab, click the Filters button. The Availability Filters window appears, where you can set filters and add your Favorite rooms (set in your Personalization settings) as a filter. You can filter by View on the View tab, and you can specify additional, more granular filters such as Features on the right. To apply your filter settings to the List or Schedule view, finish your selections in the window below and click Apply.

Tip: To revert to a non-filtered view, click the Reset button on the tab. When you have filters turned on, this is indicated at the top of the tab view. See Also: Filters and Favorites.
