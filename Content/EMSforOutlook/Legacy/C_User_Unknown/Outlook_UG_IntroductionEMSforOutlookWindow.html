Introduction to the EMS for Outlook Window

Whenever you interact with Microsoft® Outlook, to reach to add-in, open or begin booking an Outlook meeting and click the EMS for Outlook icon in the Outlook toolbar. The label may differ from the example show below due to your System Administrator's custom settings.

When you begin booking a meeting in Microsoft® Outlook and click the EMS for Outlook add-in button, a window similar to the example below appears, with the Schedule tab automatically selected. At any time during the booking process, you can click the Schedule tab to view a calendar of attendees' availability and other meetings adjacent to yours. Rooms are sorted in this view by book sequence.

See Also: Viewing the Schedule.

Concept: Tabs Refresh to Match Your Booking Template
When you choose a booking template in the Select Template field, the rooms listed in the Schedule and List tabs refresh to match the template specifications.

 

Note: Your administrator may have renamed the display items such as tab names and field labels on your installation of EMS for Outlook. Examples in this documentation may appear differently than your installed application.

Tabs On the EMS for Outlook Window

Click the List tab to see a list of only the available rooms that match your selected Template and Event Type. Rooms are sorted by availability: Days Available, then by Building, then by Book Sequence, then by Room Description.

Selected Tab

Once you select a room, the Selected tab shows all rooms associated with your meeting.

Services Tab

The Services tab may be available for requesting one or more services for the event, such as Catering, A/V Equipment, and so on. If you can request services, your administrator may have set this tab to prompt you for additional information. Once you select a room, this tab shows services available for the selected rooms, sorted by Category. To see services within a category, click on it and options will show to the right. After you select a service for an event, click Apply to add the service to the event. If your meeting includes multiple rooms, you can specify services for each one.

Billing Tab

The Billing tab may be available for providing billing and PO information. You can manually enter the billing information for the event, or you can click the Search icon look up existing billing reference and/or PO numbers.

Note: Additional, Optional Tabs May Appear.

Additional Info Tab—Your Administrator may set this tab to appear and prompt you to answer one or more questions about the scheduled event.
Help Tab—Your Administrator may set this tab to provide additional information.
